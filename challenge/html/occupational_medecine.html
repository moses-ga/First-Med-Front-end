Our focus

FirstMed is a local Urgent Care that’s focused on one thing only — healing. All of our Providers are efficient, caring, and well-versed in workers’ compensation. When injuries occur, we’ve got tele-medicine as well as walk-in capabilities at all of our locations, so your staff can receive immediate convenient care and get back to work.
